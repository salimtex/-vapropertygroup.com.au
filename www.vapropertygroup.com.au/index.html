What We Do

Real estate investment is highly complex, but it’s also highly personal.  

Every investor has a unique set of circumstances, priorities, and goals.

At VA Property Group, we start by listening and don’t subscribe to one-size-fits-all financial strategies. 

Our approach is comprehensive, nuanced, and tailored to fit your personal circumstances.

We offer distinctive solutions designed to help investors achieve their most important goals through all phases of real estate investment.

Acquisition. 
We act in your best interests to source, analyse and be the buyer’s agent on acquisitions. 

Refurbishment. 
Coordinate and manage refurbishment works to increase your property's value and income-generating potential.

Property Management. 
Full service property management or short-term rental management to ensure your property will generate strong, consistent cash flow.

Sales. 
As a licensed real estate agent, VA will act as the listing agent to sell your property and realise its maximum profit.

We are here to help you realise your vision for your investments. And we take it personally.
