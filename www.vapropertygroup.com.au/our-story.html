OUR STORY

VA Property Group is the culmination of Kate and Mark Braybrook’s two decades of experience in finance, property development, property management and construction. 

The couple’s broad and diverse skill set is the reason why VA is a one-stop shop for property investors who are frustrated with their under-performing investment portfolio.

In the early 2000s, Mark was acquiring and renovating properties and Kate was climbing the ranks in an ASX200 company as the General Manager, responsible for a $400M residential development portfolio in New South Wales and Victoria. 

In 2011, Kate and Mark decided to take advantage of a depressed global real estate economy and moved to the USA to start their own business.  The couple operated a boutique investment management company in Austin, Texas, and represented high-net-worth Australian investors looking to diversify their investments offshore and take advantage of the declining US property prices and high Australian dollar.

For five years, Kate and Mark acquired and renovated almost 100 homes and 120 apartments throughout Texas, Florida and Las Vegas, on behalf of multiple investors. As the US economy improved and offshore investment demand waned, Kate and Mark closed their business.  

Since 2016, Kate has been part of Austin’s largest urban infill developer leading their expansion into the multi-family real estate sector and was responsible for establishing the new business division and overseeing the acquisition, development and stabilisation of real estate assets for long-term income generation. 

Mark created a new business, Logic Flatpack Assembly, which predominantly installed Ikea kitchens and built it from the ground up before selling it in 2020.

Kate and Mark have been homeowners, landlords, tenants, developers and investors and understand the frustrations and headaches that an under-performing investment portfolio can provide.  Their mission behind VA Property Group is to make property investment an enjoyable and financially rewarding experience for every client.
